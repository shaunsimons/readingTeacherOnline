Welcome to Reading Teacher Online

Reading Teacher Online provides the necessary instruction for students to master the reading basics as quickly as possible. They can then benefit from their formal education and future possibilities.

Instruction is done via video and online sessions.

Language is natural, but written language is not. Written language is a skill invented by humans. It has to be taught.

Until recently, in human history, most children were not taught to read and write.

Today, even with public education, many children struggle to read and write well enough to cope with the modern world.

This is an enormous handicap.

Why do so many children struggle to learn to read and write?

Many children fail to read and write because they haven't been taught.

Today, thanks to neuroscience, we have a scientific understanding of reading. We know each child is unique, and each brain is unique.

We also know that many of our current reading theories do not align with reading research.

Your child can be taught the fundamentals of the English alphabet system in about twelve hours or less.

I teach the fundamental reading basics step-by-step and provide the graded reading texts, so they get plenty of practice that leads to fluent reading.
